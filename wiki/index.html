Welcome to the Forever RPG Wiki, the community reference for ⚔ Forever RPG — a browser-based, multiplayer-flavored RPG set in the lands of Norrath. All information is drawn from the open-source game code available on GitHub.

Races

Choose from 14 playable races — from noble Humans and wise High Elves to fearsome Ogres and regenerating Trolls. Each race brings unique base stats and available class combinations.

Classes

16 distinct classes spanning four archetypes — Melee, Hybrid, Caster, and Priest. Master the Warrior's disciplines, the Necromancer's dark arts, or the Bard's inspiring songs.

Combat System

Tick-based combat with a configurable speed slider (500–5000 ms). Pull enemies, manage your party's roles, cast spells from the hotbar, and loot fallen foes automatically.

📜 Did You Know?

  • Ogres have a base STR of 130 — the highest of any race in Norrath.
  • The combat speed slider ranges from 500 ms (lightning fast) to 5000 ms (slow and deliberate).
  • Erudites have the highest base INT (107) of any race, making them the premier spellcasters.
  • All characters start their journey in Qeynos — The City of Honor.
  • The Plane of Fear is the highest-level zone, recommended for veterans only.
  • Ghost characters persist for up to 72 hours in the world after a player logs off.
  • The Bard class can inspire allies with songs that provide ongoing buffs during combat.
  • Paladins possess the ultimate survival tool — Lay on Hands — usable once per day.
