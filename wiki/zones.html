Forever RPG is set in Norrath, a vast and ancient world of magic, danger, and wonder. The game world is divided into zones — distinct regions with their own enemies, terrain, atmosphere, and level requirements. All characters begin their journey in Qeynos — The City of Honor and venture outward from there.

World Overview edit

Norrath is a world of classic high fantasy — rolling green hills, ancient dungeons, frozen wastelands, haunted forests, and even the planes of the gods themselves. The game's zone progression leads players from the relatively safe outskirts of Qeynos into increasingly hostile territories.

Each zone has a level range indicating the recommended character levels for that area. Entering a zone above your level is possible but dangerous — enemies will be much stronger than you. The zone's xpModifier affects how much experience characters earn there.

Qeynos — The City of Honor edit

Qeynos is the hub city of Forever RPG and the starting point for all adventurers. It is a safe zone (no combat) where characters can access city services, manage their gear, trade, and socialize with other players.

The City panel (accessible from the center panel when in Qeynos) has five tabs:

TabFunction
🏦 Bank Store items you don't need in your active inventory. The bank provides essentially unlimited storage — stash crafting materials, spare weapons, or seasonal gear here.
🏪 Market The player-driven economy. List items for sale, browse other players' listings, and purchase gear. Prices are set in gold/silver/copper. Market data is persisted in localStorage and shared across sessions.
⚔️ Guild Create or manage your guild, view guild roster, and access guild-specific features. See Guilds & Community.
👥 Players Browse ghost characters from other players currently in the world. Click a ghost to open the Ghost Inspect modal and view their equipment and stats.
🏆 Rankings View the global leaderboard — top players sorted by level, kills, or achievements. Links to the standalone ranking.html page.

Zone Navigation edit

Zone Minimap

The left sidebar's Zone panel displays a minimap of your current zone — an SVG illustration showing terrain features like hills, water, roads, and points of interest. The minimap also shows your current camp location if one has been set.

Zone Information

Below the minimap, the zone panel shows the zone's name, level range, and a brief description. The top bar always shows the current zone name, keeping you oriented as you explore.

Traveling Between Zones

Zone travel is handled through the game's zone selection UI. Zones unlock progressively as your party grows stronger. Traveling to a new zone changes the available enemy pool and ambient music/color. All characters in your party move together — there is no individual zone separation.

Zone Chat

Each zone has its own chat channel visible in the Zone Chat tab of the combat log area. This is a real-time chat shared among all players currently active in that zone. See Zone Chat for more details.
