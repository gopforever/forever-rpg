Achievements

Contents

The Achievement System in Forever RPG tracks milestones across every aspect of the game — from your first kill to slaying 5,000 enemies, from visiting a new zone to equipping a full set of gear. Achievements are implemented in js/achievements.js and stored in localStorage.

Overview edit

When you earn an achievement, a toast notification appears on screen — a brief popup with the achievement name and description. Some achievements require a threshold (e.g., kill 100 enemies) while others are one-time events (e.g., first login). There are currently over 60 achievements across 9 categories.

Achievements are shared across your save — all party members contribute to a unified achievement total. World First achievements track who in the entire player community accomplished something first.

World Firsts edit

World First events are special achievements awarded to the first player in the entire community to accomplish a notable milestone — such as the first to reach level 60, the first to kill a specific named enemy, or the first to complete a full gear set.

When a World First occurs, all players currently online receive the History Maker achievement. World Firsts are announced via the World Feed in the center panel, visible to all active players.

Rankings & Leaderboard edit

The Rankings system tracks and displays the top players across several categories. Rankings are accessible from:

  • The Rankings tab in the Qeynos City panel
  • The standalone ranking.html page (linked from the top bar)

Rankings are implemented in js/ranking.js with dedicated styles in css/ranking.css. Players are ranked by various metrics including:

  • Highest character level
  • Total enemies killed
  • Total achievements earned
  • Total gold accumulated
  • Guild membership and guild rankings

Rankings use the same ghost system that powers the Who's Online and Players panels — player data persists in localStorage as ghost snapshots, refreshed every 30 seconds while online and retained for up to 72 hours offline.
